Project Overview

My friend wanted to open a gym just for girls so they could feel safe and comfortable while working out. I made this site to help her show what the gym is about.

Your Journey Starts Here

Fit & Fierce Women's Gym is dedicated to empowering women of all fitness levels to lead healthy and fulfilling lives. Whether you're just starting out or looking to take your fitness to the next level, we have the tools and support you need!

Intended Users

Girls and women who want a welcoming gym.
