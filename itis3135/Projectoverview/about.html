Meet the Founder

Fit & Fierce Women's Gym was started by a close friend named Sarah Thompson, who wanted to create a safe and welcoming space where girls and women could feel comfortable working out. Inspired by her passion for fitness and her commitment to empowering women, she built this gym to help women of all fitness levels reach their goals in a supportive and uplifting environment.
