Website Evaluations

Apple.com

A website showcasing Apple's products and services.

  • Contrast: Excellent use of contrast to highlight key information.
  • Repetition: Consistent design elements throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Precise alignment creates a clean and organized layout.
  • Proximity: Related elements are logically grouped.

Penny Juice

A website selling juice concentrates for childcare centers.

  • Contrast: Very poor contrast. Colors clash, and text is often difficult to read against busy backgrounds.
  • Repetition: No consistent design elements. Fonts, colors, and layouts change erratically.
  • Alignment: Elements are scattered haphazardly, with no clear alignment, creating a chaotic visual.
  • Proximity: Unrelated elements are often grouped, and related elements are separated, making it hard to understand the site's structure.
